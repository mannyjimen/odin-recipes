Torta Recipe

Are you Mexican? How about Hispanic! If not, the my oh my I have something to show you. Today, we will be making a delicious Torta Mexicana. Enjoy the rich lettuce, tomato, and avocado mixed in with the meat of your choice. Wrap it around two Oaxacan Buns, and you got yoursel a Torta Mexicana! AY AY AY!

Ingredients

  • Sirloin Steak
  • Garlic Salt
  • Black Pepper & Cumin
  • Kaiser Rolls
  • Mayonnaise
  • 1 Large Avocado
  • 1 Large Tomato
  • 2 Cups of shredded lettuce
  • 1 ounce crumbled cotija cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill, season the steak with all the spices in the ingredients.
  2. Grill steak on the preheated grill untill medium-rare, about 5 minutes per side.
  3. Spread both halves of each roll with Mayonnaise. Brown the rolls, and warm the refried bean in a bowl in the microwave.
  4. Spread thin layer of beans onto the bottom half of each roll, layer the steak, avocado, tomato, and lettuce. Top with cheeses, and close the sandwich. Enjoy!
