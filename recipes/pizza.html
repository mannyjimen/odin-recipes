Pizza Recipe

Ahhh, classic Italian Pizza. A real New Yorker's delight! Today, we will show you how you can make a classic cheese Pizza right at the comfort of your home. This pizza will have you screaming "I'm wawkin heere"!

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Bread Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Place warm water in bowl, add yeast and sugar and MIX!
  3. Add flour, oil, and salt to the yeast mixture; beat until smooth.
  4. Let rest for 5 minutes.
  5. Take dough, roll it into flat 12-inch circle, and transfer the dough to the prepared Pizza Pan.
  6. Put whatever the fu** you want on the pizza, and then stick it in the oven.
  7. ENJOY!
